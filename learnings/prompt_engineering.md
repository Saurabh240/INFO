"Analyze my resume for [job title]. Strengthen weak bullet points, align it with the job description, and highlight missing keywords. Also point out anything that may reduce my chances in ATS screening."

Rewrite these responsibilities into achievement-based bullet points. Use metrics and results. If I didn't provide numbers, ask me for them before rewriting.

→ The "ask me for them" part is key. It forces Claude to push back instead of inventing numbers.


𝟯. 𝗙𝗶𝗻𝗱 𝘁𝗵𝗲 𝗴𝗮𝗽𝘀 𝗯𝗲𝗳𝗼𝗿𝗲 𝘁𝗵𝗲 𝗿𝗲𝗰𝗿𝘂𝗶𝘁𝗲𝗿 𝗱𝗼𝗲𝘀

Compare my resume to this job description. List the skills I'm missing. Tell me which ones I can reframe from existing experience and which ones I need to develop.

→ This is the most underrated prompt on this list. Most people skip it.

𝟰. 𝗞𝗲𝘆𝘄𝗼𝗿𝗱 𝗼𝗽𝘁𝗶𝗺𝗶𝘇𝗮𝘁𝗶𝗼𝗻 (𝘄𝗶𝘁𝗵𝗼𝘂𝘁 𝘀𝘁𝘂𝗳𝗳𝗶𝗻𝗴)

Extract the top 20 keywords from this job description. Integrate them into my resume naturally. I don't want a list in the skills section.

→ ATS filters are real. This gets you past them without making your CV read like a keyword dump.
